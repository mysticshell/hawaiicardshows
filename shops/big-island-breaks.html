About the Shop

Card Breaks on the Big Island

Big Island Breaks is a card shop based in Kailua-Kona, bringing the hobby to Hawaii’s Big Island. Specializing in sports cards, Pokemon, and live card breaks, they offer collectors on the west side of the island a local spot to find cards, rip packs, and connect with the hobby community.

As one of the few dedicated card shops on the Big Island, Big Island Breaks fills an important gap for collectors who would otherwise have to order everything online or wait for trips to Oahu. Whether you’re looking for the latest releases, hunting for hits in a live break, or just want to browse, they’re a welcome addition to the Kona card scene.

Follow @big_island_breaks on Instagram for break schedules, new product drops, and shop updates. You can also find their links at linktr.ee/bigislandbreaks.
