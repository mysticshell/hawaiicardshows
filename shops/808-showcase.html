About the Shop

TCG, Anime & Collectibles Under One Roof

808 Showcase by Datz Collectiblez is a go-to destination for trading card games, anime collectibles, and Sanrio merchandise across Oahu. With two locations — one at Pearlridge Center in Aiea and another at Ka Makana Alii in Kapolei — they've made it easy to find what you're looking for no matter which side of the island you're on.

As an official Pokemon tournament store, 808 Showcase runs weekly Pokemon tournaments alongside One Piece and Lorcana events. They host a Tournament Night on the last Friday of each month starting at 4 PM, plus Trade Night every third Friday from 4 PM. Whether you're a competitive player or a casual collector, there's always something happening.

They buy, sell, and trade across their full inventory — from Pokemon and Yu-Gi-Oh singles to Gundam kits and plushies. With 3.8 stars on Yelp and roughly 5.5K Instagram followers, they've built a solid community following. Check @datz_collectiblez_808showcase on Instagram for the latest drops and event updates.
