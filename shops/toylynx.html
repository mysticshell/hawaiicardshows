About the Shop

Where the Competitive Players Go

ToyLynx is the tournament heartbeat of Oahu's TCG scene. Located in Honolulu right by Costco on Iwilei Rd, the shop runs organized play nearly every day of the week — from Friday Night Magic and Saturday Pokemon to Sunday One Piece and midweek Yu-Gi-Oh events. If you're looking to compete, this is where it happens.

Beyond tournaments, ToyLynx carries one of the deepest inventories on the island. Singles, sealed product, booster boxes, supplies, and Japanese imports across every major TCG. Their online storefront at Ideal808.com extends the shop's reach beyond the islands, but the in-store experience — browsing the cases, pulling from the singles wall, and getting a game in before close — is what keeps the local community coming back.

Whether you're prepping for a regional qualifier, hunting for a specific single, or just want to shuffle up and play a few casual rounds, ToyLynx has a seat for you.
