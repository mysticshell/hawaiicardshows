About the Shop

Hawaii’s Premier Card Shop

Aloha Card Shop is the go-to destination for card collectors on Oahu, with three locations spanning the island — Ward (flagship), Windward Mall, and Kahala Mall. Whether you’re hunting for Pokemon, sports cards, graded slabs, or sealed product, they carry deep inventory across every major category.

Known for knowledgeable staff, competitive prices, and being a true hub for the local collector community, Aloha Card Shop has earned a strong reputation with 4.6 stars on Yelp across 46+ reviews. They’re also an official Pokemon League location and regularly sell live on Whatnot.

Beyond retail, Aloha Card Shop runs Aloha Card Shows throughout the year as well as Hawaii Pop Con once a year. Follow @alohacardshop on Instagram for the latest updates.
