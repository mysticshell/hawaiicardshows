About the Shop

Maui's Dedicated Card Shop

yoCards is Maui's go-to card shop, located in Wailuku and known for having the biggest and best selection on the island. With 4.6 stars on Yelp, the shop has earned a reputation for well-priced items and a friendly, helpful owner.

The shop focuses on sports cards and Pokemon, stocking everything from singles (both graded and base) to sealed packs and boxes. They also carry supplies like sleeves, binders, storage boxes, and custom play mats. Break tables are available for anyone who wants to rip boxes in the shop.

yoCards is wheelchair accessible and accepts credit cards. Follow @yo.cards on Instagram for current hours and new inventory updates.
