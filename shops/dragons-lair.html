About the Shop

Central Oahu's Comic & Card Shop

Dragon's Lair is a comic and hobby shop in the heart of Mililani, owned by Luke and Randee. As one of the few shops in central Oahu, it's become a community favorite for collectors, readers, and gamers in the area.

They carry a deep selection of comics, manga, and graphic novels alongside trading card games like Magic: The Gathering and Pokemon. Add in tabletop RPGs, collectible figures, and toys, and there's something for just about every kind of hobbyist.

Known for friendly and knowledgeable staff, Dragon's Lair is the kind of shop where you can get solid recommendations whether you're picking up your first comic or looking for a specific MTG single. Follow them on Facebook for the latest updates on new arrivals and events.
