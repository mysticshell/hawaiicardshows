About the Shop

A True OG in the Hawaii Card Scene

Paula's Sports Cards Etc. is Hawaii's oldest card shop and a true legend in the local collecting community. Paula started doing card shows at Moiliili Community Center back in 1988, opened her first retail shop on Kinau Street in 1990, and moved to the current Makiki Shopping Village location in 1993.

With a specialty in packs, boxes, and cases, Paula's is the go-to destination for collectors who want sealed product at every level. Whether you're looking for a single pack to rip or a full case of the latest release, Paula's has the inventory and the knowledge to back it up.

Follow @paulas808 on Instagram for new product arrivals, restocks, and shop updates.
