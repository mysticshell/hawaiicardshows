Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
Where are card shows in Hawaii?+
Hawaii has regular card shows across Oahu including the Paradise Card Show, the Pearlridge Show every 2nd Saturday and Sunday, Uncle Tony's Trade Night at Pearlridge every 3rd Friday, and the Bayview Night Market on the 1st and 3rd Tuesdays. Annual events include the Aloha Card Show at Blaisdell Center and PopCon. Check our calendar above for the complete list with dates and venues.
When is the next Pokemon card show on Oahu?+
Oahu has multiple Pokemon and trading card shows every month, ranging from weekly trade nights to large annual conventions with 50+ vendors. Our calendar stays up to date with every known show across the islands — scroll up to see what's coming next.
How do I become a vendor at a Hawaii card show?+
Most Hawaii card shows accept vendor applications through online sign-up forms or direct contact with the organizer. Each show in our calendar includes organizer details. Vendor table fees typically range from $100–$200 depending on the show. If you're new to vending, smaller trade nights are a great way to start.
Are there card shows on Maui, Big Island, or Kauai?+
While most regular card shows are currently on Oahu, events do happen on the neighbor islands. We track TCG events across all Hawaiian islands. If you know of a show on any island, submit it to our calendar and help the community stay connected.
How do I submit my card show to this calendar?+
Scroll to the "Submit Your Event" section on this page and fill out the form. Our team reviews every submission and adds approved events to the calendar, usually within 24–48 hours. It's free and open to any organizer running a TCG or collectible event in Hawaii.
What kinds of cards and collectibles are at these shows?+
Hawaii card shows feature a wide range of collectibles including Pokemon, sports cards (baseball, basketball, football), Yu-Gi-Oh, One Piece, Dragon Ball, Japanese imports, vintage cards, sealed product, graded slabs, and accessories. Some shows also include comics, Funko Pops, and other pop culture collectibles.
About Us
Built for the Community

Hawaii Card Shows is a community resource built by collectors, for collectors. Our goal is simple: make it easy for anyone in Hawaii to find their next card show, trade night, or collectible event — no matter which island you're on.

Whether you're a seasoned vendor, a weekend collector, or someone who just pulled their first pack, there's a seat at the table for you. We track shows across all islands so you never miss an event.
