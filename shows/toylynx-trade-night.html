About the Event

The Island's Weekly Trading Hub

Every Thursday, ToyLynx opens its doors for Trade Night — a casual, community-driven event where collectors come to buy, sell, and swap cards across every major TCG. No vendor tables, no entry fee, no setup — just people and their binders.

Whether you're trying to complete a set, unload extras, or just browse what people are carrying, Trade Night is the most accessible way to plug into Honolulu's card scene on a weeknight. The vibe is relaxed, the crowd is consistent, and new faces show up every week.

Since it's hosted inside ToyLynx — one of the island's deepest TCG shops — you can also shop singles, sealed product, and supplies before or after trading. It's a full evening for anyone who collects.
