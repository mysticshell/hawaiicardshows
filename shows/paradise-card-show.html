About the Show

Same Stage, Different Energy

The Republik is known as one of Honolulu's go-to venues for live music, comedy, and events that pack the house. Paradise Card Show takes that same energy and builds it around trading cards — turning a space built for crowds into Oahu's biggest recurring card show.

With 50+ vendors under one roof, Paradise brings together collectors and dealers across Pokemon, One Piece, Dragon Ball, Yu-Gi-Oh, Japanese imports, graded slabs, sealed product, and more. Whether you're chasing a specific chase card, building out a binder, or just browsing to see what catches your eye, the variety runs deep.

The show is organized by Javin and a dedicated team who've built something the island's TCG community keeps coming back to. The atmosphere is easy — no pretension, no gatekeeping. Just good cards, good people, and a venue that knows how to hold a crowd.
